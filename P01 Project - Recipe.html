Chocolate Chip Pancake Recipe

The richest, fluffy, and chocolatey panckaes that you will ever consume.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 ounces semisweet chocolate chips
  • Butter, for cooking

Directions

  1. In a small pan, combine the butter and milk. Place over low heat just until warm and the butter is melted.Then in a bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt; mix well.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs. Whisk in the milk mixture. Add the dry ingredients and mix just until barely blended. Add the chocolate chips and mix.
  3. Heat a griddle or large skillet over medium heat. Add about 1 teaspoon of butter and melt until bubbly. Ladle 3 tablespoons of batter for each pancake onto the hot surface and cook until bubbly on the top and golden brown on the bottom. Turn and cook until golden brown on the other side, about 30 seconds more. Repeat until all the batter is used up. Serve hot.
